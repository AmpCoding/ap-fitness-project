INITIAL ASSESMENT

The initial process is as follows: Once payment is made, I will ask for your height, weight and age to calculate your DCE (Daily Caloric Expenditure) and devise a meal plan for your fitness journey. Next, I will need 4 current pictures of all angles (front, back and both sides) wearing a bikini or underwear and sports bra emailed to APFitnessStudio@gmail.com. I will assess where you are today and determine what you need to do to get to your body goals. You will then receive a workout regimen designed specifically for YOU! This is the personal touch I offer for all of my clients.

FOOD PLAN

A meal guide with a specified nutritional plan for you! I will help you learn how to meal prep which truly is a key aspect of the success you will achieve during your fitness journey. I will tell you when it's time to make any adjustments to your food, so no more guess work or wondering what to eat once you leave the gym or while planning your day. I cater to meat lovers, vegans, vegetarians, and pescatarians. All are welcome!

FITNESS REGIMEN

A workout regimen including Plyometrics, Cardio, and Strength Training. I determine what you need based on your personal fitness capabilities. As you improve and your body changes, I will provide you with my suggested additions to keep your program challenging and interesting all while your body continues to transform!
